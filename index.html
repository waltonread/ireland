Holyoke to Co. Kerry

SUNDAY, MARCH 16TH - TUESDAY, MARCH 18TH, 2025

Itinerary

Starting on Sunday, March 16th, 2025, a dedicated contingent from Holyoke, Massachusetts, will journey to County Kerry for an enriching cultural exchange. Our preliminary itinerary outlines three exciting days: a warm welcome and arrival on Sunday, vibrant St. Patrick’s Day festivities on Monday, and a day of cultural exploration on Tuesday, March 18th. Please note that this schedule is provisional, with some details still being finalized.

  • 6:00pm - 7:00pm: Delegation arrives in Co Kerry.
  • Civic Welcome: Sit down meal at the Tralee Bay Wetlands with Irish music and more.
  • Attendees: Tralee MD Elected Members, St. Patrick’s Day Committee, and invited guests.
  • Activities: Presentations and formal speeches.

  • 9:00 - 10:00: Meet with the St Patrick’s Day Committee for refreshments (pastries, sausage rolls, scones, etc.) at the Grand Hotel.
  • 11:00 - 13:00: St. Patrick’s Day Parade with Grand Marshall Mayor Garcia and the Holyoke Parade Committee.
  • Heritage Trail following the parade: Tralee Heritage Trail.
  • Post-parade refreshments (venue TBC).

  • 11:00 - 15:00: Site visit to the Bakery School and tour of Blennerville Windmill.
  • Round table discussions with Kerry County Council at The Rose Hotel.
  • Presentations:
    • Windmill Bakery School Update
    • Tralee Urban Regeneration Project
  • Lunch at The Rose Hotel, followed by a walk through Town Park and a tour of the Kerry County Museum.
